# Template: Evolution (Single Car, Multiple Generations)

## When to Use
- One car across 2+ generations
- 'How it improved' story
- Engineering milestone narrative
- End-of-production or rebirth story

## Structure
1. **Hook** (≤10 words, first 3 seconds): Create tension or question
2. **Previous Weakness** (5s): Set up the starting point / weakness
3. **3 Improvements** (15s): Core evolution beats
4. **Number Impact** (5s): Key stat that lands the point
5. **Cultural Reaction** (5s): How the world responded
6. **CTA** (5s): Binary question to drive comments

## Example
R32 → R33 → R34 → R35: "Godzilla evolved"

## Scoring Checklist (6 items, must score ≥4)
1. Hook creates tension or curiosity
2. At least 2 generations clearly compared
3. Technical specs mentioned
4. Numbers are accurate and cited
5. CTA invites engagement
6. Under 160 words total
