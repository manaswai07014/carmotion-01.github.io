# Template: Comparison (Two Generations Head-to-Head)

## When to Use
- Direct two-generation rivalry
- Fan community debates ("which is better?")
- Cross-brand same-era comparison
- "R33 vs R34" type queries

## Structure
1. **Hook** (≤10 words, first 3 seconds): Create rivalry tension
2. **Spec Flash Compare** (10s): Quick side-by-side numbers
3. **Key Divergence** (15s): Where they differ most
4. **Verdict** (5s): Clear winner or draw
5. **CTA** (5s): Binary choice question

## Example
R33 vs R34: "Which is the real Godzilla?"

## Scoring Checklist (6 items, must score ≥4)
1. Hook creates clear rivalry tension
2. At least 3 specs compared directly
3. Key divergence clearly explained
4. Numbers are accurate and cited
5. CTA invites debate in comments
6. Under 160 words total
