# Tesla — Brand Overview

> Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded in 2003. Known for pioneering modern electric vehicles, Tesla produces the world's best-selling EVs.

---

## Brand Identity

• **Founded:** 2003 | San Carlos, California, USA
• **Founder:** Martin Eberhard & Marc Tarpenning
• **CEO:** Elon Musk (2008–present)
• **Stock:** NASDAQ: TSLA
• **代表顏色:** Tesla Red -> #CC0000
• **官方網站:** https://www.tesla.com

---

## Notable Models

### Passenger Vehicles

**🅿️ Node 1: Roadster (2008–2012)**
- First mass-produced EV sports car
- 248-288 hp | 0-100 3.7-3.9 sec
- Based on Lotus Elise chassis
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla_Roadster_(2008)

**🅿️ Node 2: Model S (2012–2026)**
- Full-size luxury sedan
- 362-1,020 hp | 0-100 1.99-5.2 sec
- Motor Trend 2013 Car of the Year
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla_Model_S

**🅿️ Node 3: Model X (2015–2026)**
- Mid-size luxury SUV with falcon-wing doors
- 670-1,020 hp | 0-100 2.6-6.0 sec
- Seating for 6-7 passengers
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla_Model_X

**🅿️ Node 4: Model 3 (2017–present)**
- Compact executive sedan
- 283-450 hp | 0-100 3.3-5.6 sec
- World's best-selling EV (2018-2020)
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla_Model_3

**🅿️ Node 5: Model Y (2020–present)**
- Compact crossover SUV
- 283-456 hp | 0-100 3.7-5.0 sec
- World's best-selling vehicle (2023)
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla_Model_Y

**🅿️ Node 6: Cybertruck (2023–present)**
- Full-size electric pickup
- 350-845 hp | 0-100 2.6-6.5 sec
- Stainless steel exoskeleton design
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla_Cybertruck

### Commercial Vehicles

**🅿️ Node 7: Semi (2022–present)**
- Class 8 electric semi-truck
- Up to 800 kW (1,072 hp)
- 325-500 mile range
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla_Semi

## Key Innovations

• **Electric Powertrain:** Pioneered modern EV with usable range and performance
• **Supercharger Network:** Proprietary high-speed charging infrastructure
• **Autopilot/Full Self-Driving:** Advanced driver assistance technology
• **Over-the-Air Updates:** Remote software improvements
• **Megacharger:** High-power charging for Semi (1.2 MW)
• **4680 Battery Cells:** Tabless battery technology for improved efficiency

---

## Sales Milestones

• 2021: Model 3 becomes first EV to pass 1 million global sales
• 2023: Model Y becomes world's best-selling vehicle
• 2023: Tesla delivers 1.8 million vehicles globally
• 2024: Cumulative EV sales exceed 5 million

## Production Facilities

• **Fremont Factory** — Fremont, California (Model S, X, 3, Y)
• **Gigafactory Shanghai** — Shanghai, China (Model 3, Y)
• **Gigafactory Nevada** — Storey County, Nevada (Batteries, Semi)
• **Gigafactory Texas** — Austin, Texas (Model Y, Cybertruck)
• **Gigafactory Berlin** — Grünheide, Germany (Model Y)
